Summary *Inception* is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating dreams. When offered a chance to clear his criminal record, he must achieve the impossible task of planting an idea—known as "inception"—inside a target’s subconscious mind.
